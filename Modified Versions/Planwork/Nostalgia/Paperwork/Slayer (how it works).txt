slayer - 
First you go to the slayer master. There, you can buy a Desert Lizard Task. This gives you access to Desert Lizards. The key disapears on use.The desert lizards drop one out of three tasks. From there, you can get better and better tasks.Sometimes, the slayer monsters drop "interesting bones" (Slayer points) You can use that to buy better equipment and eventually the slayer helmet and cape.
