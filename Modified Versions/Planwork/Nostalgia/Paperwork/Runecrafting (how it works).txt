Runecrafting -
Pure ess gives 2x runes (members)
Talismans are gotten from The Abyss. Ancient druids there drop talismans. Talismans can be traded.
Omini talisman - At the air alter, you can trade your Omini Talisman (0) for an Omini Talisman (1). It goes on and on until you have an Omini Talisman which, at the Runecrafting Guild, goves access to a room which has a teleport to each alter. Runecrafting Guild can be entered by making the teleport. Runes can be exchanged for Runecrafting Tokens.
