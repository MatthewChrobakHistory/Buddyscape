The Grand Tree

Location: Forrest

	The Grand Tree stopped working. Who is causing this to happen? Talk to the gnome king to find out.

-requires
	- Combat XP

	The Gnome king says the found that a gnome called Glough is casting black magic to wreak havok among the gnomes. Stop him!

	- Go outside the gnome's village
	- Find Glough's cave
	- Kill his summoned demon
	- Kill Glough

	The King thanks you and lets you use the grand tree now that the black magic isn't being cast anymore.

REWARDS

	- Grand Tree Access
