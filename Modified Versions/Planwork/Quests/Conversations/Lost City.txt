Lost City

Location: Planes

	A bunch of warriors say they found the lost City! Can you get them to teach you how to find it?

-requires
	- Crafting XP
	- Combat XP

	The warriors tell you to go to Entranna and make a Draven Staff.
	
	- Go to Entranna
	- Find Tree
	- Ghast Appears
	- Kill Ghast
	- Cut Tree
	- Make staff

	You learn a spell that lets you teleport to the Lost City. Well done!
