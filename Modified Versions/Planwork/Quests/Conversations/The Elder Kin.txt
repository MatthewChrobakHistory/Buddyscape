The Elder Kin

Location: Tzhar City (under island)

	The Tzharr are testing the warriors of their city.

-requires
	- Combat XP

	There is a new "boss" in town, and he's challenging all life forms to a test of endurance. Only those who beat TzTok-Jad may face him.

	- Give Firecape to the Challenger. He will let you go into the caves now. To make sure you weren't lucky in the fight caves, you are given a smaller test.

		- Endure through all seven waves and you finish the quest

	The Tzhar find you worthy enough to face the Elder Kin.
