Let them Eat Pie

Location: Home

	A rich barron is taking all the food from a chef. Teach him a lesson!

-requires
	- FishingXP
	- CookingXP

	The chef recommends making the barron so sick of food. You can do this by making a disgusting crayfish pie.
	
	- Catch Crayfish
	- Give it to the chef
	- Cook the pie
	- Give it to the barron

	The barron no longer likes food. Job well done!

REWARDS

	- Cooking XP
	- Fishing XP
