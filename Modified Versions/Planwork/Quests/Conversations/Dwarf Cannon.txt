Dwarf Cannon

Location: Mountains

	A dwarf asks you if you wish to aid them in their attack against the goblins by creating a dwarven cannon

-requires
	- Smithing XP
	- Mining XP
	- Crafting XP


	The dwarves need steel bars to make the seperate parts of the cannon. You also need to make the molds to forge the pieces.

		- Mine Clay -> Make Dwarven Cannon Molds
		- Make steel bars
		- Make cannon pieces with the parts
		- Bring the cannon pieces to dwarven outpost

	The dwarves thank you and give you a golden pickaxe in exchange.

REWARDS

	- SmithingXP
	- Golden Pickaxe
