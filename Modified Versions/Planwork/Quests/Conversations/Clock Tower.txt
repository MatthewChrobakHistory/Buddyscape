Clock Tower

Location: Home

	Brother XYX's clock tower is broken and he needs you to help fix it.


	Cogs can be found in the cellar bellow the tower. There are some creatures there, so be careful! Brother XYX lost four cogs, so find all of them!

		- Find Cogs
		- Give them to Brother XYX

	Give the Cogs to Brother XYX and he'll give you a "Finished Quest" scroll.

REWARDS

	Coins
