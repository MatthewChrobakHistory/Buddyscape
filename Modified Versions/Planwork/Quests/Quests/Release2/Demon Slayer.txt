Demon Slayer

Location: Home

REQUIREMENTS
	
	Combat XP



	A gypsy tells you that you are destined to defeat Delerith, a demon coming to the world. You need Silverlight to kill him!

	The gypsy says that silverlight was owned by King Arthur, but he lost the sword in the cave where delerith would appear should he return. Find the sword!


	Go to the cave and find the sword. Then go into the next room and defeat delerith.

	Defeat the enemies, and complete the quest!
