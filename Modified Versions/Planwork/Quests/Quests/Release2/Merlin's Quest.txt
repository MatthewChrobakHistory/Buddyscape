Merlin's Quest

Location: Home

REQUIREMENTS
	
	Miningxp (Optional)
	Smithingxp (Optional)
	Combatxp

	King Arthur asks you if you want to be a knight of the round table. If yes, you need to prove yourself! There's a band of unruly bandits in an outpost of King Arthurs. Defeat them with the Excalibur!


	Excalibur can be purchased at the lady of the lake for a steel sword.

		- Make/Purchase sword
		- Sword is blessed by the lady of the lake

	Defeat the enemies and their leader, and complete the quest!

REWARDS

	Excalibur
